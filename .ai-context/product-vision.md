# StreamPicks Product Vision

## What StreamPicks is

StreamPicks is a creator-based competitive game.

It should feel like a mix of:
- fantasy sports
- creator prediction
- social rivalry
- daily progression
- public flexing

The mobile app is the main gameplay surface.

## Core emotional loop

The core loop is:

discover creators -> draft/pick -> sweat the countdown -> daily reveal -> rank movement -> flex/share -> return tomorrow

This loop is more important than any single isolated feature.

## What makes the product compelling

StreamPicks is strongest when it emphasizes:
- creator heat and momentum
- daily reveal drama
- rankings and tier progression
- rivalry and social pressure
- teams as identity groups
- visible improvement and flex moments
- premium tools that feel powerful and desirable

## Product philosophy

Free should make users care.

StreamPicks+ should help them:
- win more intelligently
- organize more deeply
- flex harder
- express status and identity

## The app versus the website

The app is where the real game lives.

The website should support the app by:
- explaining the product quickly
- making it feel exciting and premium
- catching shared traffic
- preparing for future logged-in pages
- improving conversion into the app and StreamPicks+

The website should not be treated as a full gameplay mirror in the current phase.
