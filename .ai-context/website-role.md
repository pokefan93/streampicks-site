# Role of the Website

## What the website should do

The website is a companion surface for the StreamPicks ecosystem.

Its main jobs are:

1. Explain the game clearly and quickly.
2. Make StreamPicks feel exciting, competitive, and socially alive.
3. Increase app installs and early user conversion.
4. Support future share landing pages and account-linked surfaces.
5. Support future premium conversion.

## What the website is not

The website is not currently meant to be:
- the full game in a browser
- a full account dashboard
- a full league management surface
- a complete duplicate of the app

Those may become future layers, but not in the current phase.

## Website tone

The site should feel:
- modern
- energetic
- competitive
- creator-native
- premium
- socially alive

It should not feel:
- corporate
- cold
- overexplained
- generic
- like a design-agency brochure
