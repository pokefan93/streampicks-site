# Constraints and Non-Goals

## Constraints

- Do not overengineer the website.
- Do not assume the website should mirror the full app.
- Do not change the stack unnecessarily.
- Do not add heavy dependencies without a strong reason.
- Preserve working legal/footer/static pages unless intentionally improving them.
- Keep the site mobile-friendly.
- Avoid breaking the current page structure unless there is clear value.

## Non-goals right now

These are not the current priority:
- full browser gameplay
- full league management on web
- full rankings backend on web
- complex dashboard features
- major backend refactors
- introducing lots of new tools just because they are modern

## Product guardrails

Free should carry the core fun.
Premium should carry the extra power, prestige, and deeper tools.

The website should support:
- understanding
- trust
- excitement
- conversion
- future compatibility

The website should not become bloated or confusing.
